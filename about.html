Our Story

Celebrating fire, flavour, and heritage

Afrikas Tapas is a proudly African meat-smoking and tapas-style food brand that celebrates fire, flavour, and heritage. Rooted in traditional African cooking methods and reimagined for modern tastes, we specialise in slow-smoked meats, bold spices, and curated small-plate experiences designed for sharing and connection.


Our approach blends ancient techniques with contemporary presentation, delivering food that is both nostalgic and forward-facing. Each dish is carefully prepared using quality cuts, slow smoking, and flavour profiles created in our Southern African kitchen.
