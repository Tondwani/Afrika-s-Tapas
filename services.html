Our Services

Bringing authentic African smokehouse to your event

Afrikas Tapas operates across pop-ups, private events, brand collaborations, and curated food experiences, offering customised menus that cater to intimate gatherings as well as large social occasions.


The brand is driven by a passion for storytelling through food - where smoke, fire, and time come together to honour culture while exciting modern palates.

Corporate Catering

Minimum: 10 persons


Elevate your corporate events with authentic African smokehouse cuisine. Perfect for:

  • Team building events
  • Product launches
  • Client entertainment
  • Office celebrations
  • Conference catering
  • End-of-year functions

We bring premium quality and professional service to businesses of all sizes, creating memorable experiences that leave a lasting impression.

Festival & Market Stalls

Bringing the authentic African smokehouse experience to festivals and markets. We specialize in:
